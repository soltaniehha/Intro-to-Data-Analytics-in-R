Answer:

Part 1)

The relationship differs by cylinder number. While there is no reasonable relationship between cyt and disp for 5 cylinder cars, it is negative for 4 cylinder cars, less pronounced but still negative for 6 cylinder cars and no relationship or very small postive relationship for 8 cylinder cars.

Part 2)

2seater cars have larger engines but smaller body weight by design. As a result they are more fuel efficient. This is causing our fit to have slightly positive slope. If we were to exclude those few datapoints we could still get a negative relationship.
